# OperateAI — IG / Stories pack (6 pieces)

On-brand vertical Story graphics (1080×1920) + copy. Voice: blunt, plain-English,
en-AU. Funnel: every piece points to the free **AI Visibility Check** (`/check/`).
Graphics rendered from `stories.html` using the live design tokens (Signal lime
`#f3fc85` on charcoal/paper, Hanken Grotesk + JetBrains Mono).

**How to post each:** upload the PNG to an IG Story → add a **Link sticker**
(`operateai.com.au/check`) over the lime pill → add the suggested interactive
sticker. The pill text already tells people what to tap. Repostable to a feed
carousel or Reel cover too.

**Cadence:** one every 2–3 days, rotate the hooks. Lead with #1 (it's interactive
= most reach). Close a "campaign week" with #6 (the offer).
